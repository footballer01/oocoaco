G.G.I.S(Goel Ganga International School)

Welcome

Ranked amongst the top 10 CBSE schools of Pune, GG International School, Pimpri is an ISO Certified, co-educational Senior Secondary School in which has been in existence since 2007.. With an excellent track record of 100% result in CBSE board exams for class 10 th and 12 th for 12 consecutive years, our football, taekwondo, basketball, swimming and athletics champions make our believe stronger that within each student lies a player and an erudite soul. Founded under the aegis of Goel Ganga Foundation, the school and foundation envision of serving each stratum of society with quality education and aiding the development of the society with its resource and expertise. The foundation along with the school has taken up many community outreach projects such as Adopting a Tribal Village, Installing an Advanced Solar Panel System in School, Creating a Mini-Forest in School (Aaranya), Visiting orphanages, paraplegic centres, cleaning of ghats during Ganpati Festival, celebrating Joy of Giving week etc. Our Mahatma Gandhi Centre, ATAL Tinkering Lab gives each student a platform to explore their potential and work towards Swatmanan Bodh. We believe in offering each child a pathway to success – academically, physically, emotionally and socially. The school has 4 branches – Pimpri,Bavdhan,Chinchwad and Rahatani
